About Us

From industrial design to IT, we are a team spanning a diverse range of identities, industries, educational backgrounds, & working experience; who believe that when it comes to breaking into a new field, you don't have to do it alone.

Amidst the lingo & jargon, the software & tools of the trade, technical interview preparation, salary negotiation, and everything in between, we get it: it can be daunting to dive head first into the design world. We often found ourselves asking questions like, "What is the difference between a UX designer, web designer, product designer, graphic designer?" or "What exactly does a UX researcher or an information architect do?"

After participating in a cohort as a part of a UX/UI bootcamp course within Columbia Engineering's curriculum, we found that we were stronger when we worked together. A common goal of ours soon became to provide budding designers like ourselves with an accessible & unintimidating resource hub, community, and network to start their careers from scratch: chock-full of user-generated content, brought to you from our trusted & knowledgable mentors and peers.
